January is one of the best times to visit Phuket. Expect average daytime temperatures of 24°C – 32°C, slightly cooler evenings, very low rainfall (January is in Phuket's dry season), and moderate humidity. Overall, you can expect hot, sunny days and warm nights – perfect for an outdoor festival.

Lightweight clothing suitable for tropical weather, comfortable footwear (part of the site is grassland, part concrete), sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat for daytime sets, a light jacket or cover-up for evenings, swimwear if you're planning to enjoy Phuket's beaches or hotel pools, a reusable water bottle (empty on entry) to refill at water stations, a portable power bank for your phone, and valid photo ID (required for entry).
